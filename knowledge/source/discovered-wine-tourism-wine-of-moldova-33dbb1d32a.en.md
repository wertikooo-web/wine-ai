A tourist to the Republic of Moldova will come across genuine, warm, and friendly people and wherever such meetings occur they also offer a visitor the opportunity to enjoy the traditional food of Moldova. Wine tourism experiences in Moldova include the famous underground wine cellars of “Cricova” and “Milestii Mici”, the latter being included in the Guinness Book of Records for the largest wine collection in the world.
