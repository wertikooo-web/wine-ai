The Chateau Cristi vineyard is located in the south of Moldova, in a distinctive terroir with poor clay and limestone soils and a microclimate favorable for growing grapes. On 130 hectares, both international and autochthonous grape varieties are cultivated.

The winery emphasizes full quality control from vineyard to bottle — using only grapes from its own plantations, with control at every stage from variety selection to harvest.

Wine lines: Chateau Cristi Sparkling (in development), Chateau Cristi Family Reserve, Chateau Cristi, Bravoure Chateau Cristi, Petit Chateau Cristi, Politika Chateau Cristi.

Heritage: the winery presents itself as continuing the Cristi family's winemaking traditions across generations, maintaining a meticulous approach to production.

International recognition: wines have received awards at prestigious competitions including Decanter World Wine Awards, Mundus Vini, and Berliner Wine Trophy.
