The institution was designed to conform with the model of similar wine-growing institutions in the world’s major wine-producing countries. The ONVV was established based on the Government Decision no. 725 of September 16, 2013 and implementation of sectoral policies started in April 2014 The establishment of ONVV was a decisive step taken by the public and private sector in light of the implementation of changes in wine sector legislation, the normative framework and of the promotion of the country’s wine brand: “Wine of Moldova. A legend alive”. The purpose of the ONVV is to contribute to the recognition and promotion of quality Moldovan wine abroad. The ONVV operates under the Ministry of Agriculture and Food Industry, and performs as a public-private partnership, being managed, and financed jointly from the Vine and Wine Fund, set up out of contributions from the private sector and state budget allocations. The Vine and Wine Fund is not a legal entity. It was created with a specific objective, namely with the purpose to finance measures aimed at the development of the wine sector in strict compliance with the Law on Vine and Wine no. 57-XVI of March 10, 2006. The resources of the Fund are accumulated in the treasury account of the National Office of Vine and Wine. The resources of the Fund are used in strict compliance with the annual budget of the Office and its activity plan, approved by the Coordination Council, for the following actions: Promotion of wine products’ export, research, and analysis of marketsConducting training programmesDevelopment and implementation of projects, investment programmes and technical assistance in the wine sectorRealisation of viticulture and wine growing development programsConducting sector analysesDevelopment and implementation of programmes of conformity and quality assurance for wine productsManagement of wine production with protected geographical indication, protected designation of origin and country brandTechnology and know-how transferProvision of consultancy to the wine sectorRemuneration of staff and financing of current expenses related to the statutory activity of the OfficeThe ONVV keeps accounting and statistical records of the Fund resources in line with the Law. The report on the annual budget of ONVV illustrates the revenues accumulated in the Fund by category of sources and the expenses incurred by the Fund on items of expenditure for the reporting period. The control of the legality of the operations, procedures and the use of the Fund resources is performed by the state authorities.
