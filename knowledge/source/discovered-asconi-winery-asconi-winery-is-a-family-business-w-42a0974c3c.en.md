Asconi WineryWine, food, and hospitality runs through the veins of every Moldovan, however for us it has a separate place in our hearts. For us this is more than just a business, it’s the fruit of our passion and hard work. Every single bottle, dish and the entire atmosphere within, is created with love. We strive for the highest quality of wine, mouthwatering meals and warmest welcomes. As Vince Lombardi once said “Perfection is not attainable but if we chase perfection we can catch excellence!” DISCOVER THE WINERY Family PassionAsconi Winery is a family business founded in 1994, which aims at producing the best possible wine, using the latest technology, whilst maintaining the traditions of the local people. As a family business, Asconi Winery is a home, the local people are our community and the local traditions our heritage, thus we are excited to share our home with you. Visit our winery, discover our wines, taste our food and share our success and accomplishments with us, because no matter where you are from, you will always have a home at Asconi Winery. DISCOVER THE WINERY “Perfection is not attainable but if we chase perfection we can catch excellence!”Vince Lombardi The WineryThe foundation of our wines is built on French varietals such as Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot, Chardonnay, Sauvignon Blanc and Muscat. But we have also planted other varieties such as Riesling, Malbec, Pinot Noir and many more.By 2024, we have planned to replace some of the existing vines and also add a few more varieties to our humble collection. The GuesthouseIn the summer of 2019, we have opened a tourist guesthouse inside our winery, which consists of 12 rustic cottages (20 rooms), with traditional Moldovan architecture and a thatched roof, made by hand. For your comfort, each cottage has a flat-screen TV, safe, personal care items, hairdryer, minibar with homemade snacks, ASCONI wines, and other facilities that will make you feel at home. And what can you enjoy more than a flower and vegetable garden right next to your house? Not to mention the vine alley, which will capture your attention, is a perfect background for photos. The RestaurantsWith the aim of showcasing the most authentic Moldavan traditions, the winery’s first restaurant, ASCONI, brings you the true heritage, tastes and aromas of Moldova. Together with our kitchen team, we have researched our families’ recipes and compiled our favorites, bringing you authentic tastes, cooked with fresh ingredients and sprinkled with love, just like Grandmother used to make it! SOL NEGRU restaurant Moldovan with a twist balkan is served under a hand-crafted thatched reed roof and paired with your favourite ASCONI wines. SOL NEGRU focuses on easy dishes to share with your family and friends, easy conversation starters, comforting your soul with farm to table vegetables, home-made cheeses and breads and memorable flavors.
